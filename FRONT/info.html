About This Site

Sleek, minimalistic and simple Rainbow Six Siege player finder. Quickly access the most important statistics, and share them easily with either screenshot or link.

Solo project created by me. Created it as a long time player of the game, for fun, to level up my programming skills. Open source here too. If you are here, thanks for using the tracker.
